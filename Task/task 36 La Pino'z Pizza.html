The Story Of La pino'z Pizza

-----

La Pino’z is a pizza restaurant chain founded in 2011 by Sanam Kapoor in his hometown of Chandigarh, India. With the goal of providing fresh, customizable pizza options for everyone, La Pino’z has since grown to expand to 500+ outlets across India and most recently one in London.Born from a desire to offer something different and better than the traditional pizzeria, La Pino’z was started with the ambition of giving customers exactly what they wanted with each visit. Now, after almost 10 years of hard work and dedication, La Pino’z is still going strong, delivering made-to-order pizzas all around the world.
